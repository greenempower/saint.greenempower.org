Saints of The Green Empower Organization

Saints directory of The Green Empower Organization.

In my mind we will define and designate saints (or some iteration of concept).

Our saints will be people who take our values core values and live then.

I think we should try to avoid ranking saints in terms of their innate abilities.

More important is that they are take our values to heart and live them.

I'm thinking that our sainthood should be more of an internal characterization rather than something we try to put on people who may not be interested.

I Holden Green at this time would like to make myself a saint of Green Empower.
